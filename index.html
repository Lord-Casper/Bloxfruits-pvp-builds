Top 15 Best Non-Braindead Builds for Beginner PvP in Blox Fruits

1. E-Claw Rumble Yama Kabucha

This build is quite versatile with great potential in both air and ground-based PvP. Adding cyborg makes it overpowered. The stats you need are Melee, Defense, and Sword. There are many one-shot combos with this build, making it good for dealing with teamers, runners, and air campers.

2. Superhuman Rumble Soul Cane Acidum Rifle

This build works well with cyborg, and is made specifically for 1v1s, making it perfect for bounty hunting when there is only one opponent. It excels in both land and air combat. The Acidum Rifle moves are easy to hit, making the combos easy to execute. You can obtain this build in the 2nd sea, and it can fence a 30 million bounty quite fairly.

4. Superhuman/Godhuman Spirit Spikey Trident Soul Guitar/Kabucha

This build depends heavily on the user's experience due to its extensive list of combos that are effective but difficult to land. With enough skill, you can become a true "Raging Demon."

5. E-Claw Rumble Bisento-v2/Spikey Trident Kabucha

This build is special because it is both easy to hit and deals significant damage. It can one-shot a 30 million bounty, as per my experience. This build is underrated by many but has helped many players reach 30 million bounty with ease.

6. Sharkman Karate Magma Kabucha

This build excels in dealing area damage and crowd control. Sharkman Karate provides powerful close-range attacks, while Magma adds high damage over time. Ideal for taking on multiple opponents.

7. Death Step Phoenix Dark Blade

This build offers excellent mobility and healing capabilities, making it ideal for sustained combat. The combination of Death Step's damage and Phoenix's healing allows for prolonged engagements.

8. Dragon Talon Flame Soul Guitar

A highly aggressive build that focuses on burst damage and quick kills. Dragon Talon's high damage output combined with Flame's crowd control abilities can quickly overwhelm opponents.

9. Electric Claw Buddha Spikey Trident

This build is tanky and can absorb a lot of damage while dishing out powerful counterattacks. Buddha's transformation increases defense and attack range, making it hard for enemies to approach.

10. Cyborg Fishman Karate Soul Cane

A balanced build with a focus on defense and utility. Cyborg's energy shield reduces incoming damage, while Fishman Karate and Soul Cane provide reliable damage and crowd control.

11. E-Claw Light Shisui

This build is perfect for hit-and-run tactics, utilizing Light's speed and E-Claw's combo potential. Shisui adds extra damage and quick attacks, making it a solid choice for PvP.

12. Dragon Talon Venom Dark Blade

This build combines Venom's damage over time with Dragon Talon's burst damage, creating a deadly combination. Dark Blade provides high damage output, making it effective in taking down opponents quickly.

13. Superhuman Dough Kabucha

Superhuman and Dough together create a highly versatile build that excels in crowd control and damage. Kabucha adds range attacks, making it easier to handle multiple opponents in PvP.

14. Death Step Ice Pole

This build focuses on freezing opponents with Ice's control abilities and dealing massive damage with Death Step. The Pole's reach allows for effective mid-range combat, making it a balanced build.

15. Electric Claw Phoenix Rengoku

Combining Phoenix's healing and mobility with Electric Claw's fast attacks makes this build very resilient in PvP. Rengoku adds powerful sword attacks, ensuring high damage output.
